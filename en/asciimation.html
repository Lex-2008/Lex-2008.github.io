Star Wars in ASCII art

One guy has too much of free time :) He created asciimation.co.nz (cached version) - a website where you can watch a big part of Star Wars episode IV (the first movie) carefully printed as ASCII art animation.

That's pretty impressive by itself, but especially if you read FAQ (cached) and think about all the effort gone into making this.
