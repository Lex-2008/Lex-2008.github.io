Custom domain email providers

Created: 2014-05-22 — modified: 2021-07-02 — tags: links e-mail

If you happen to own a domain name and want to have an email there – these guys are here to help you!

These providers support English interface and have a free option:

  • Yandex Mail

These providers support English interface, but don't have a free option:

  • Google Apps
    • forbids attachments with specific extensions
    • not all email clients play nice with Google's vision of IMAP prtocol
  • Microsoft Office 360, FastMail, FlaskMail, ProtonMail, Pobox, rackspace, bigrock, Atmail, Tutanota, Zoho

These providers support only Russian interface, but have a free option:

  • Mail.Ru for business
    • Unlimited storage

If you don't have a domain name - there are many places where you can get free domain, most known of them is dot.tk which gives second-level domians ending with .tk
