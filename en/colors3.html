A nicer version of CSS-only 2D background gradient

Compare background of this page with the previous version and enjoy the progress of CSS!

background:
	linear-gradient(
		to bottom in hsl,
		rgba(255,0,255,0.5),
		rgba(255,255,0,0.5),
		rgba(0,255,255,0.5),
		rgba(255,0,255,0.5)),
     linear-gradient(to right in hsl, #f00, #0f0, #00f);
